About us

In the year of 1920, in the height of the cars, a group of visionary Honduran and Indian businessmen and car lovers, understood that cars would be the future and decided to join to constitute a company whose objective would be to provide quality maintenance to the same.

Many years later this company is still standing; Times have changed, but the goal remains the same. Using experience and a rich tradition, Mr. Wash continues to constantly reinventing itself, to always be a synonym for excellence and responsibility, a leader in car maintenance.
